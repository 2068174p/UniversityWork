Wine merchant sells bottles of wine at a variety of
prices on a sale or return basis.
• Customers are allowed to trade on credit
• Wine merchants require an interface that will allow
– enter details of a series of sales and/or returns
– update the customer balance as appropriate
• Sale/return involves one or more bottles of a single type
of wine, giving the cost for one bottle
Interface should display cost of sale (or amount to be
refunded) and total cost of all sales and returns to date.
On running program, customer’s name and initial
balance are to be obtained via two input dialog boxes
(from the JOptionPane class).
